# Investor Profile: Tech & Macro Momentum
**Sectors of Interest:** Artificial Intelligence, Semiconductors, Cloud Computing, Biotech.
**Market Cap Focus:** Mid-cap to Mega-cap ($2B+).
**Key Signals:** - Earnings per share (EPS) beats or misses.
- Upgraded or downgraded forward revenue guidance.
- Significant insider buying (Form 4s).
- Macroeconomic shifts (Federal Reserve interest rates, CPI data).
**Objective:** Identify high-conviction bullish or bearish catalysts that will move the market at the opening bell. Ignore generic market noise, minor price fluctuations, and penny stocks.
